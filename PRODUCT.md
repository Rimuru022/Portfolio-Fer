# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters evaluating remote engineering talent, and prospective clients seeking automation consulting. They are busy, scanning quickly for credibility and proof. The ideal user is a decision-maker who values results over buzzwords and wants to see that the person behind the site is both technically capable and trustworthy enough to hand a project to.

## Product Purpose

A personal brand portfolio that converts attention into consulting leads and remote job offers. It exists to prove, in seconds, that Fernando is an excellent, precise, and adaptable engineer who has delivered real results at scale (Huawei cloud infrastructure, Castrol/BP automation and BI). Success looks like inbound consulting inquiries and recruiter outreach.

## Brand Personality

Ambitious. Adaptable. Precise.

Voice is confident but never arrogant. Entrepreneurial energy without the hustle-culture noise. Every claim is backed by evidence. The tone says: "I have done the work, here is the proof, let me do it for you."

## Anti-references

- Generic portfolio templates (Bootstrap, Tailwind UI cookie-cutter layouts)
- Overly corporate or stiff "resume website" aesthetics
- Unprofessional or playful gimmicks (memes, excessive animation for its own sake)
- SaaS hero-metric clichés (big meaningless numbers with gradient text)
- "AI slop" interfaces that feel generated rather than considered

## Design Principles

1. **Show the work, don't just list it.** Proof lives in data, images, and case-study depth. Every project section must demonstrate outcome.
2. **Precision is the personality.** Alignment, spacing, and typography are exact. Sloppiness destroys trust.
3. **Trust through transparency.** No vague claims. Metrics, tools, timelines, and scopes are stated clearly.
4. **Structured but not rigid.** The layout is organized and scannable, yet adapts to the story of each project.
5. **Entrepreneurial confidence.** Bold headings, decisive color choices, and generous whitespace signal someone who owns their craft.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA target. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. Ensure color contrast passes for common color-blindness types (protanopia, deuteranopia). Keyboard-navigable project carousels and case-study sections.
